How much time does the average person spend online/in front of a screen?

According to one study, the average person spends about 9 hours of their day looking at a screen. That's more than 1/3 of their day!

Why does tech feel so addicting?

Things like autoplay, notifications, and the way tech is designed make it more engaging to use and get us hooked!

What can I do if I feel I'm using my phone too much?

Consider trying to limit the 'passive' ways you use tech(like having videos constantly come on on autoplay. Consider also finding momenets in the day to do other fun activities like meeting with friends, reading or taking walks/exercising if you can.
